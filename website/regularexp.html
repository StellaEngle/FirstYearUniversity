Regular Expressions

Building Patterns

Square brackets (Match any one of the characters or ranges in the brackets)

  • [ae] matches one of a or e.
  • [a-z] matches any one of the lower case letters
  • [0-9] matches any one of the digits

Caret negates a range (Matches anything but..)

  • [^a-z] anything but the lower case letters
  • [^1-3] anything but the digits 1, 2, 3

Escape special characters with \

  • [\\[\\]] matches opening or closing square bracket
  • [\.a-z] matches a dot followed by a single lower case letter
